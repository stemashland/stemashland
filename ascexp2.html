Welcome to Ashland STEM Club

Ashland STEM is a locally run club held on Saturdays at 11:00am during the summer, from July through August, at the local library in the recreation room. Parents are welcome to stay.

Contact us: stemashland@gmail.com
Mission Statement: Our STEM club aims to provide an engaging and educational experience for kids (K-5) interested in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. We offer hands-on activities, experiments, and projects that spark curiosity and foster a love for STEM subjects.

Location: Local library, recreation room
