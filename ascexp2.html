Welcome to Ashland STEM Club!

Ashland STEM Club is dedicated to fostering a love for **Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics** in young minds. We believe in learning by doing, offering engaging hands-on activities, exciting experiments, and collaborative projects that spark curiosity and build essential skills. Our mission is to empower the next generation of innovators right here in Ashland!

We are a locally run club, passionately committed to providing an inclusive and educational experience for children in grades **K-5**. Join us for an unforgettable journey of discovery!

Our Vision:

  • To make STEM accessible and exciting for all elementary-aged children.
  • To cultivate critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity.
  • To build a supportive community where kids can explore, experiment, and grow.
  • To inspire future scientists, engineers, and tech leaders.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What are the club hours for Summer 2025?

A: The club will run every Saturday from July 5th to August 30th, 2025, from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM.

Q: Is there an age or grade limit for joining?

A: Yes, Ashland STEM Club is designed for children in grades K-5 (kindergarten through fifth grade).

Q: Can parents stay during the sessions?

A: Absolutely! Parents are always welcome to stay and observe, or even participate in the activities with their child. We encourage parent engagement!

Q: What is the cost of the program, and what does it include?

A: The full Summer 2025 program (9 sessions) costs $180 per child. This fee covers all experiment materials, supplies, and an exclusive Ashland STEM Club t-shirt.

Q: How do I register my child?

A: Please fill out the "Registration Form" on our Join Us! tab. We will then send you a confirmation email with payment details.

Q: Do you offer sibling discounts?

A: Yes, we offer a 10% discount for siblings enrolled in the same summer program. Please contact us after submitting your registration forms for details on how to apply the discount.

Q: What should my child bring to the club?

A: Just their enthusiasm and a curious mind! All materials for experiments will be provided. You may want to bring a water bottle.
